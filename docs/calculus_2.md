## More about e

$$ e = (1 + dx)^{\frac{1}{dx}} = \lim_{\Delta x \to 0} (1 + \Delta x)^{\frac{1}{\Delta x}} = \lim_{N \to \infty} (1 + \frac{1}{N})^N $$

$$ e^x = \lim_{N \to \infty} (1 + \frac{1}{N})^{Nx} = \lim_{N \to \infty} (1 + \frac{x}{Nx})^{Nx} $$

$$ Nx \to \infty $$

$$ N \to \infty $$

$$ e^x = \lim_{N \to \infty} (1 + \frac{x}{N})^{N} $$
